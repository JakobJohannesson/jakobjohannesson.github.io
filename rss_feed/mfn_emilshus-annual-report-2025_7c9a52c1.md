**About Emilshus****
**Emilshus is a property company rooted in the business culture of Småland that acquires, develops and manages high-yield commercial properties, with southern Sweden as its core market. The company’s property portfolio at December 31, 2025 totaled 1,188 ksqm of leasable area distributed among 233 properties with an emphasis on light industry, industrial services/trade suppliers and big-box and grocery retail. Emilshus’s ordinary share and preference share are listed on Nasdaq Stockholm.
