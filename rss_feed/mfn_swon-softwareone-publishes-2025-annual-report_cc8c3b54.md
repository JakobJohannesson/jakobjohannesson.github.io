About SoftwareONE 

SoftwareOne is a global software and cloud solutions provider and distributor.
With a presence in over 70 countries and a team of about 13,000 professionals,
we combine global scale and local expertise to help partners and
customers optimize costs, source and procure, accelerate growth, and navigate
complex IT environments with confidence. Leveraging deep capabilities in cloud,
software, and data and AI, the company empowers organizations to modernize,
innovate, and unlock the full value of their technology investments.
Headquartered in Switzerland, SoftwareOne is listed on the SIX Swiss Exchange
and Euronext Oslo Børs under the ticker symbol SWON.
