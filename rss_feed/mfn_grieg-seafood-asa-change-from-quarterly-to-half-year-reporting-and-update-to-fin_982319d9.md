# Grieg Seafood ASA: Change from quarterly to half-year reporting and update to financial calendar

**Published:** 2026-03-31 18:50:00
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/a/grieg-seafood/grieg-seafood-asa-change-from-quarterly-to-half-year-reporting-and-update-to-financial-calendar](https://mfn.se/a/grieg-seafood/grieg-seafood-asa-change-from-quarterly-to-half-year-reporting-and-update-to-financial-calendar)
**MFN ID:** grieg-seafood
**Company:** Grieg Seafood

---

Grieg Seafood ASA (the "Company") has resolved to change its financial reporting frequency from quarterly reporting to half-year reporting.

The change reflects the Company's current size and the potential for short-term fluctuations to cause volatility in quarterly results. The Company considers half-year reporting to provide a more appropriate basis for communicating the underlying development of the business over time.

The Company will nonetheless continue to keep the market informed on a quarterly basis by publishing an extended trading update following the end of each quarter, including key operational and selected financial highlights. The Company will, however, no longer publish full quarterly interim financial reports for the first and third quarter (Q1 and Q3). Full interim financial reporting will be provided for the first half-year (H1) and the full year, in accordance with applicable requirements.

**Revised financial calendar**

As a consequence of the change, the Company's financial calendar is updated as follows:- Q1 2026 - Extended trading update: approx. 21 May 2026, please be referred to our website for exact date- H1 2026 - Half-year report: 27 August 2026- Q3 2026 - Extended trading update: approx. 12 November 2026, please be referred to our website for exact date

**About Grieg Seafood ASA**

Grieg Seafood ASA is one of the world’s leading salmon farmers. Our farms are located in Rogaland in Norway with our headquarter in Bergen, Norway. Grieg Seafood ASA was listed at the Oslo Stock Exchange in June 2007. Sustainable farming practices are the foundation of Grieg Seafood's operations. The lowest possible environmental impact and the best possible fish welfare is both an ethical responsibility and drive economic profitability.

To learn more, please visit [www.griegseafood.com](http://www.griegseafood.com).
