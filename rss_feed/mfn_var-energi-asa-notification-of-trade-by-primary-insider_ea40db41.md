# Vår Energi ASA: Notification of trade by primary insider

**Published:** 2026-03-30 13:42:30

**

Sandnes, Norway, 30 March 2026: Deputy employee representative to the Board, Kjersti Elen Aanderaa in Vår Energi has sold 8 200 shares in the company at a price of NOK 47.88 per share. The transaction was performed on 20 March 2026. Following the transaction, Kjersti Elen Aanderaa holds a total of 3 743 shares in Vår Energi ASA.**

Details of the trades are included in the attached notification of trading. This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to MAR article 19 and Section 5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
