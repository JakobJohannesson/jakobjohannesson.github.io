# EQL Pharma CEO sells shares to finance capital gains tax from option redemption

**Published:** 2026-03-27 16:48:50
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/cis/a/eql-pharma/eql-pharma-ceo-sells-shares-to-finance-capital-gains-tax-from-option-redemption-aeb583b1](https://mfn.se/cis/a/eql-pharma/eql-pharma-ceo-sells-shares-to-finance-capital-gains-tax-from-option-redemption-aeb583b1)
**MFN ID:** eql-pharma
**Company:** EQL Pharma

---

**EQL Pharma CEO Axel Schörling has recently sold shares for approximately SEK 1.2 million to finance capital gains tax that arose upon redemption of the option program in September 2025.**

Axel Schörling has sold 24,066 shares and holds 389,892 shares in EQL Pharma after the sale. The sale corresponded to 5.8% of Schörling's total holdings.
