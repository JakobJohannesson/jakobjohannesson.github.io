# Image Systems AB: Image Systems' Business Unit Motion Analysis Receives SEK 1.0 Million Order from a Leader in Automotive Safety in the U.S.

**Published:** 2026-03-31 16:56:23
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/cis/a/image-systems/image-systems-ab-image-systems-business-unit-motion-analysis-receives-sek-1-0-million-order-from-a-leader-in-automotive-safety-in-the-u-s-30a30f74](https://mfn.se/cis/a/image-systems/image-systems-ab-image-systems-business-unit-motion-analysis-receives-sek-1-0-million-order-from-a-leader-in-automotive-safety-in-the-u-s-30a30f74)
**MFN ID:** image-systems
**Company:** Image Systems

---

Image Systems AB's Motion Analysis business unit has received an order from a leader in automotive safety in America. The order, estimated at SEK 1.0 million, highlights the ongoing demand for sophisticated motion analysis solutions within the automotive safety sector.  

The order relates to software subscription of the company's products with a contract period of 36 months. 

"This order from an automotive safety leader highlights the value our technology brings and the industry's trust in us."

says Andreas Ovemyr, CEO of Image Systems Motion Analysis.
