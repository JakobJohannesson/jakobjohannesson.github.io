# Sinch AB: Change in the number of shares and votes in Sinch

**Published:** 2026-03-31 09:30:00
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/cis/a/sinch/sinch-ab-change-in-the-number-of-shares-and-votes-in-sinch-0db80c5b](https://mfn.se/cis/a/sinch/sinch-ab-change-in-the-number-of-shares-and-votes-in-sinch-0db80c5b)
**MFN ID:** sinch
**Company:** Sinch

---

**Stockholm - March 31, 2026 - Sinch AB (publ) today announces that the number of shares and votes in Sinch amounts to 771,740,885.**

The change in the number of shares and votes is a result of 39,604 shares being issued in March due to the exercise of stock options under incentive programs.
