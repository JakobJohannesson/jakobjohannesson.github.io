# Change in the total number of shares and votes in Flerie AB (publ)

**Published:** 2026-03-31 08:00:00
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/a/flerie/change-in-the-total-number-of-shares-and-votes-in-flerie-ab-publ-1](https://mfn.se/a/flerie/change-in-the-total-number-of-shares-and-votes-in-flerie-ab-publ-1)
**MFN ID:** flerie
**Company:** Flerie AB

---

**The total number of shares in Flerie AB (publ) (the “Company”) as of 31 March 2026 amounts to 87,601,573, all of which are ordinary shares. There are no shares issued of series C. The total number of votes in the Company as of 31 March 2026 amounts to 87,601,573.**

The change in the number of shares and votes in the Company is due to (i) the issuance of 2,187,051 ordinary shares in the directed share issue resolved by the board of directors on 24 February 2026, and (ii) the issuance of 7,958,560 ordinary shares that constituted merger consideration in connection with the completed merger with Lipum AB (publ).

**About Flerie**

Flerie is an active long-term life science investor, with a broad and diversified portfolio of innovative companies based on pioneering science. We invest in product development and commercial growth opportunities globally alongside other leading investors, focusing predominantly on private companies that are otherwise difficult to access. Flerie’s active ownership model, broad network and resources support and accelerate the development of the portfolio projects, creating value for shareholders. Flerie AB’s ordinary share is listed on Nasdaq Stockholm with the ticker FLERIE. For further information please visit [www.flerie.com](http://www.flerie.com/).
