Tieto Corporation      STOCK EXCHANGE RELEASE      31 March 2026 08:00 a.m. EEST

** **

Tieto has today cancelled 1 390 000 treasury shares in line with the decision on the share buyback programme, announced on 12 February 2026. The purpose of the share buyback programme of EUR 150 million is to maintain an efficient capital structure of the company in line with the company's capital allocation principles. The company will cancel the repurchased shares on a monthly basis. The cancellation reduces the total number of shares and votes to 117 250 150. 

 

The cancellation of the shares was registered with the Finnish Trade Register on 31 March 2026, after which the company holds a total of 656 693 treasury shares, including the shares repurchased on 30 March 2026.
