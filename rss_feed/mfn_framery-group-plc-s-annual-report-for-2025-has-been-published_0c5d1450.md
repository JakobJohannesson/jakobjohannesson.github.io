Framery Group Plc | Stock Exchange Release | March 31, 2026 at 09:00:00 EEST

**Framery Group Plc’s annual reporting package for 2025 has been published.**

The reporting package includes a review of Framery’s year, the Report of the Board of Directors and the Financial Statements, as well as the Corporate Governance Statement and the Remuneration Report for 2025. The Report of the Board of Directors includes the company’s first Sustainability Statement in accordance with the EU Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD), prepared following the European Sustainability Reporting Standards (ESRS). The audit firm Ernst & Young Oy has provided a limited assurance report on the Sustainability Statement.

The Consolidated Financial Statements are published in accordance with the European Single Electronic Format (ESEF) reporting requirements as an xHTML file, and the primary statements have been marked with XBRL tags.

All reports are available as PDF files attached to this release and on the company’s website at[](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://framery.com/en/investors/)[https://framery.com/sijoittajat/en/reports-and-presentations/](https://framery.com/sijoittajat/en/reports-and-presentations/) The Annual Report is also attached as an xHTML file.

**Framery in Brief**
Framery enables people to focus on what truly matters and to get things done. With its soundproof smart pods and smart office solutions, Framery turns ordinary offices into places people love. This is why Framery is an essential part of a successful workday for millions of workers in over a hundred countries and within many of the world’s leading companies.
