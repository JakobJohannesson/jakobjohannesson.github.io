# Manufacturer of UHP gas and chemical delivery systems has become part of Atlas Copco Group

**Published:** 2026-04-02 15:00:00
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/cis/a/atlas-copco/manufacturer-of-uhp-gas-and-chemical-delivery-systems-has-become-part-of-atlas-copco-group-3323625b](https://mfn.se/cis/a/atlas-copco/manufacturer-of-uhp-gas-and-chemical-delivery-systems-has-become-part-of-atlas-copco-group-3323625b)
**MFN ID:** atlas-copco
**Company:** Atlas Copco

---

**Nacka, Sweden, April 2, 2026: The assets related to the Stainless Design Concepts ("SDC") division of CVD Equipment Corporation ("CVD"), a New York State company designing and manufacturing ultra-high purity gas and chemical delivery systems, has become part of Atlas Copco Group.**** **

SDC is based in Saugerties, NY State, US, and manufactures gas and liquid chemical delivery and distribution systems, designed for semiconductor and industrial applications. The company has 26 employees who have joined Atlas Copco Group as part of the acquisition.  

The purchase price amounts to approximately 16.9 MUSD.

SDC has become part of the Semiconductor Equipment division within the Vacuum Technique Business Area.
