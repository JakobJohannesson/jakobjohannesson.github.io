Hillerstorp, Sweden, 1 April, 2026

PRESS RELEASE

**Troax Group today published its Annual and Sustainability Report for 2025. Martin Nyström, President and CEO, summarised the year: **

"2025 was characterised by weak demand and a challenging market environment, which impacted both sales and profitability. At the same time, we took important steps to strengthen Troax for the future - we completed three strategic acquisitions, improved the efficiency of our production and supply chain, and made progress in the area of sustainability, including increasing the share of renewable energy to nearly 35%. The fact that we also celebrated 70 years as a company and ten years on the stock exchange reflects the stability we continue to build on. We enter 2026 with a stronger platform and are well positioned to deliver profitable growth when the market turns." 

 
The Annual and Sustainability Report is available at www.troax.com. Currently, only the Swedish version is available - the English report will be published on 16 April.
