At a Board meeting of Karnov Group AB (publ), the Annual and Sustainability Report for the financial year 2025 has been presented and approved. The Annual and Sustainability Report also includes the Corporate Governance Report for the financial year 2025. **

The Annual and Sustainability Report is available on [https://www.karnovgroup.com/en/financial-reports-and-presentations/](https://www.karnovgroup.com/en/financial-reports-and-presentations/). The Annual and Sustainability Report in accordance with European Single Electronic Format (ESEF) is available in Swedish. 

The Annual and Sustainability Report is also available in the company's main office and will be distributed to those who have requested a printed version. To order a printed version, please send your name and address to [erik.berggren@karnovgroup.com](mailto:erik.berggren@karnovgroup.com).
