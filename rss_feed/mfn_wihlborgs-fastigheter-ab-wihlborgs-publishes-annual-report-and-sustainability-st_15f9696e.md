# Wihlborgs Fastigheter AB: Wihlborgs publishes Annual Report and Sustainability Statement for 2025

**Wihlborgs Fastigheter AB's Annual Report and Sustainability Statement for 2025 has been published and is available in Swedish and English on the company's website.**

In her CEO statement, CEO Ulrika Hallengren highlights the growing strategic importance of the Öresund region, Wihlborgs' strong earnings, and the company's ability to adapt to a changing business environment.

"In 2025, we set new records for rental income and our income from property management increased 11 percent, adjusted for changes in value in joint ventures. It shows that our strategy of long-term focus on the right locations and the right projects leads to stable, sustainable growth despite caution in some segments of the lettings market."

At the same time, she emphasizes the company's operational strength during a period of changing tenant needs:

"This has been tested by the changing needs of several major tenants in recent years. Our strength has been in quickly managing these changes in a structured manner, while simultaneously developing the properties, strengthening our areas' attractiveness and creating growth in rent levels. Net lettings during the year were positive, with SEK 77 million for the full year."

Wihlborgs is not subject to the Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD), but strives to meet increasing expectations regarding transparency, comparability, and reliable sustainability information. For the 2025 financial year, the company has therefore chosen to apply condensed and stakeholder-adapted sustainability reporting - a hybrid model based on the structure and data points of the European Sustainability Reporting Standards (ESRS), focusing on what is most relevant to the company's operations and stakeholders. During the year, new sustainability targets were also developed, which will take effect starting in 2026.

The report is available for download at [https://www.wihlborgs.se/en/investor-relations/reports/](https://www.wihlborgs.se/en/investor-relations/reports/). A summary is available at [https://www.wihlborgs.se/en/investor-relations/annual-report/](https://www.wihlborgs.se/en/investor-relations/annual-report/).
