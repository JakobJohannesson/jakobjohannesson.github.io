JPMorgan Chase & Co. ("JPMC ") has a notifiable interest in the share capital of
Hexagon Composites ASA as a result of trades by its subsidiary J.P. Morgan
Securities PLC.  The total number of shares held by JPMC on 30 March was
16,545,670 being 6.564% of the issued share capital and voting rights of the
company. The holding in JPMC crossed a notification threshold of 5% on 30 March
2026.
