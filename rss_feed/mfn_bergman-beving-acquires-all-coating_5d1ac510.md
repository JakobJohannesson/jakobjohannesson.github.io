Uveco, a company within the Machinery & Equipment division, has today acquired all shares in All Coating Industrilackeringar AB. Under the product brand Flexcoat, the company offers sheet metal in customer-specific colours based on a proprietary method that allows the sheet metal to be further processed afterwards. All Coating is based in Gothenburg, has 5 employees, and generates approximately SEK 25 million in revenue with very strong profitability.

*“All Coating is a strong and profitable company that complements Uveco well. The acquisition strengthens our offering and creates clear opportunities for profitable growth through cross-selling and a broader customer offering,” *says Per Johnsson, CEO of Uveco.

*“Becoming part of Uveco and Bergman & Beving provides us with a strong platform for our next growth phase. Together, we can broaden the offering, reach more customers, and continue developing with solid profitability,” *says Peter Berntsson, CEO and co-owner of All Coating.

All Coating will be included in the Machinery & Equipment division as a subsidiary of Uveco. The acquisition takes effect immediately. It is expected to have a marginally positive impact on Bergman & Beving’s earnings per share during the 2026/2027 financial year.
