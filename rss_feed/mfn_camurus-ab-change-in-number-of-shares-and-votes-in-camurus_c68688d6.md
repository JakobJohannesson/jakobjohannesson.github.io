# Camurus AB: Change in number of shares and votes in Camurus

**Published:** 2026-03-31 07:00:00
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/cis/a/camurus/camurus-ab-change-in-number-of-shares-and-votes-in-camurus-7cb940c0](https://mfn.se/cis/a/camurus/camurus-ab-change-in-number-of-shares-and-votes-in-camurus-7cb940c0)
**MFN ID:** camurus
**Company:** Camurus

---

**Lund, Sweden - 31 March 2026 - **During March 2026, 54,450 employee stock options in Camurus' employee stock options program ESOP 2022/2026 have been exercised for subscription of the corresponding number of registered shares.

As a result of the exercises made under ESOP 2022/2026, the number of shares and votes has increased by 54,450.

Prior to the exercises of employee stock options, the total number of shares in Camurus amounted to 59,934,734 corresponding to 59,934,734 votes. As of 31 March 2026, the total number of shares in Camurus amounts to 59,989,184 corresponding to 59,989,184 votes.

**About Camurus**
