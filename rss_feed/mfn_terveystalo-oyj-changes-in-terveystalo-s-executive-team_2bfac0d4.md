Terveystalo Plc stock exchange release 31 March 2026 at 9:00 a.m. EET

Two Senior Vice Presidents of the Healthcare Services business area have been appointed to Terveystalo's Executive Team, effective 1 April 2026. This business area is a key part of Terveystalo's strategy and generates almost 80% of the Group's total revenue. The Healthcare Services business area is headed by President and CEO Ville Iho.	- **Niki Kotilainen, **Senior Vice President of Operations, Healthcare Services, b. 1985, M.Sc. (Tech.). Kotilainen has held several leadership roles at Terveystalo since 2015. Prior to joining Terveystalo, Kotilainen worked as a management consultant at Boston Consulting Group.	- **Tiina Tissari**, Senior Vice President, Consumer, Insurance and Top specialities, b. 1973, M.Sc. (Tech.), Ph.D. Tissari has been in her position since 2024 and is also responsible for the Group's brand, marketing, digital sales, and customer experience. Prior to joining Terveystalo, Tissari worked as Finnair's Vice President, Consumer Business and Customer Experience, founded and led the sustainable fashion clothing company Vestiarium, and held various management positions at Microsoft and Nokia.

Both will report to CEO Ville Iho. 	- I warmly welcome Niki and Tiina to the Group's Executive Team. The appointments, together with our new management model, strengthen our ability to respond to the needs of our customers and professionals and support the implementation of our strategy. Our renewed management model enables closer cooperation and better decision-making throughout the Group," says **CEO Ville Iho**.

At the same time, the Group's medical management approach will be reformed. In the future, the chief physicians of the business areas will be responsible for medical management in their respective areas and will form Terveystalo's highest medical decision-making body, the Medical Management Team, whose chairman will also participate in the Group Management Team meetings. At the same time, the position of the Group's Chief Medical Officer will be discontinued. The new management model enables the effective integration of medical decision-making into business support, improving the ability to meet the needs of both customers and professionals. Jukka Pitkänen, Chief Medical Officer of the Healthcare Services business area, will assume the role of Chairman of the Medical Management Team. In the future, CEO Ville Iho will also serve on the medical management team, bringing him even closer to medical decision-making.
