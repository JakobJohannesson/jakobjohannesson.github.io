**Stockholm - Tele2 AB ("Tele2") (Nasdaq Stockholm: TEL2 A and TEL2 B) will publish its first quarter 2026 results on Wednesday, 22 April 2026. The presentation will be hosted by Jean Marc Harion, President and CEO, and Peter Landgren, EVP Group CFO.**

Tele2 will publish its financial results for the first quarter of 2026 on Wednesday, 22 April 2026, at 07:00 CEST (06:00 BST, 01:00 EDT).

**Webcast and teleconference**
Tele2 will host a webcast with presentation and teleconference on Wednesday, 22 April 2026, at 09:00 CEST (08:00 BST, 03:00 EDT). The presentation will be held in English.  

Registration for the webcast and a separate registration for the teleconference will be available at [tele2.com](http://www.tele2.com/).

For the teleconference, please note that dial-in numbers and unique PIN codes (or a "call-me" function) will be provided upon registration at [tele2.com](http://www.tele2.com/). To make sure you are connected in time, please register at least a few minutes in advance.
