# Change in number of shares and votes in Alligator Bioscience AB

**Published:** 2026-03-31 15:00:00
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/a/alligator-bioscience/change-in-number-of-shares-and-votes-in-alligator-bioscience-ab-8](https://mfn.se/a/alligator-bioscience/change-in-number-of-shares-and-votes-in-alligator-bioscience-ab-8)
**MFN ID:** alligator-bioscience
**Company:** Alligator Bioscience

---

**During March 2026, the number of shares and votes in Alligator Bioscience AB (“Alligator Bioscience”) has increased as a result of the exercise of warrants series TO 14, issued in connection with the rights issue of units carried out during October – December 2025.**

As of 31 March 2026, the total number of outstanding shares and votes in Alligator Bioscience amounts to 628,106,848, whereof all outstanding shares are ordinary shares with one vote each.
