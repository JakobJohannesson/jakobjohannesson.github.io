**Hamilton, Bermuda, April 1, 2026**

Himalaya Shipping Ltd. (the “Company”) has previously announced on March 12, 2026, that it has scheduled its 2026 Annual General Meeting for May 20, 2026. A copy of the Notice of Annual General Meeting and Form of Proxy, and associated information including the 2025 Annual Report on Form 20-F is attached to this press release and can be found on the Company’s website at [www.himalaya-shipping.com](http://www.himalaya-shipping.com/).
