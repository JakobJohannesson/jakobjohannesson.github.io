ANNOUNCEMENT NO. 80 - 1 APRIL 2026

On 5 February 2026, NORDEN initiated a share buy-back programme in accordance with Regulation No. 596/2014 of the European Parliament and Council of 16 April 2014 (MAR) and Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2016/1052, (Safe Harbour regulation). The share buy-back programme runs from 5 February 2026 up to and including no later than 30 April 2026. For details, please see announcement no. 30 of 4 February 2026.

 

Under the share buy-back programme, NORDEN will purchase shares for up to a total of USD 25 million (approximately DKK 158 million). The following transactions have been made under the programme:
