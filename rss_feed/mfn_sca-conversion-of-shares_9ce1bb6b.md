# SCA: Conversion of shares

**Published:** 2026-03-31 08:00:00
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/cis/a/sca/sca-conversion-of-shares-2e7fc3aa](https://mfn.se/cis/a/sca/sca-conversion-of-shares-2e7fc3aa)
**MFN ID:** sca
**Company:** SCA

---

**In March, at the request of shareholders 1,600 Class A shares were converted to Class B shares. The total number of votes in the company thereafter amounts to 1,250,125,959.**

The total number of registered shares in the company amounts to 702,342,489 of which 60,864,830 are Class A shares and 641,477,659 are Class B shares.
