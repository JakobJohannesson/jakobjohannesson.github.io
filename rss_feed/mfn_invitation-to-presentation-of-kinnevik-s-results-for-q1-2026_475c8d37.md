**Kinnevik AB (publ) ("Kinnevik") will publish its financial results for the first quarter of 2026 on Thursday 16 April 2026 at 08.00 CET. A conference call will be held on the same day at 10.00 CET to present the results.**

The conference call will be held in English. Those who wish to participate and ask questions during the conference call are welcome to register via the link below. Participants will receive dial-in numbers and confirmation code upon registration. Please make sure to register and dial in a few minutes before the call begins.- Link to the listen-only webcast: [https://edge.media-server.com/mmc/p/vu6jkkt7](https://edge.media-server.com/mmc/p/vu6jkkt7)- Link to the conference call for participants: [https://register-conf.media-server.com/register/BIf8cb316e1fd7442eac0146639ac44347](https://register-conf.media-server.com/register/BIf8cb316e1fd7442eac0146639ac44347)

Earlier today, Kinnevik held a pre-close call with sell-side analysts covering movements in public market inputs relevant to the valuation of Kinnevik’s private portfolio. The materials for this call are available on the Investor Relations section on Kinnevik’s website.

Kinnevik is an active owner and partner, providing patient capital from our own balance sheet to technology-enabled growth businesses. Our founders are building tomorrow’s industry leaders, making everyday life easier and better for people around the world. We invest across all stages of a company's growth journey, focused on creating long-term shareholder value. Kinnevik was founded in 1936 by the Stenbeck, Klingspor, and von Horn families. Kinnevik's shares are listed on Nasdaq Stockholm’s list for large cap companies under the ticker codes KINV A and KINV B.
