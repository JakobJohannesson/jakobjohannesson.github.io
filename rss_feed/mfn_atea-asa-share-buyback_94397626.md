Oslo, 27 March 2026

Please see below information about transactions made under the share buyback
program for Atea ASA.

Date on which the buyback program was announced: 18 August 2025.

The duration of the buyback program: 18 August 2025 to 30 April 2026, or until
the maximum number of shares has been repurchased.

Size of the buyback program: Up to 800,000 shares

From 19 March 2026 until 27 March 2026, Atea ASA has purchased a total of 35,000
own shares at the Oslo Stock Exchange at an average price of NOK 138.18 per
share. Following the completion of the above transactions, Atea ASA owns a total
of 1,060,974 own shares, corresponding to 0.94% of Atea ASA's share capital.

This is information that Atea ASA is obliged to make public pursuant to the EU
Market Abuse Regulation and subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to
Section 5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

Appendix:
A detailed overview of all transactions made under the buyback program that has
been carried out during the above-mentioned time period is attached to this
report and available at www.newsweb.no.
