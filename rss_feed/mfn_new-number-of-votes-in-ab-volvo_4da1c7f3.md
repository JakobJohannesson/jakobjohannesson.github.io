# New number of votes in AB Volvo

**Published:** 2026-03-31 13:00:00
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/cis/a/volvo/new-number-of-votes-in-ab-volvo-f19a8622](https://mfn.se/cis/a/volvo/new-number-of-votes-in-ab-volvo-f19a8622)
**MFN ID:** volvo
**Company:** Volvo

---

**The number of votes in AB Volvo has changed due to the conversion of a total of 745,007 Series A shares to a total of 745,007 Series B shares. The conversions were implemented with the support of the opportunity for Series A shareholders to request conversion of Series A shares to Series B shares which was entered in the Articles of Association at the 2011 Annual General Meeting.**

Today, 31 March 2026, the last trading day of the month, there are a total of 2,033,452,084 registered shares in AB Volvo. Of these are 441,543,462 Series A shares and 1,591,908,622 Series B shares. The number of votes in the company amounts to 600,734,324.20 based on the number of registered shares.
