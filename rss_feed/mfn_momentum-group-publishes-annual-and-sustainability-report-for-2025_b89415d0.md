**Momentum Group has today published its Annual and Sustainability Report for 2025. The report is available on the Group’s website.**

President and CEO Ulf Lilius comments in the CEO statement:“Momentum Group demonstrated stability in 2025 despite a cautious industrial market and an uncertain environment. Revenue and earnings increased, cash flow was strong and we completed six acquisitions that further strengthen the Group’s position. At the same time, we achieved our target of doubling EBITA already four years after our listing in 2022. With a clear strategy, strong entrepreneurs in our companies and a business model that combines the development of existing businesses with acquisitions, Momentum Group is well positioned for the next step in its growth journey.”

The Annual and Sustainability Report for 2025 is available for download in [English](https://www.momentum.group/en/report/annual-and-sustainability-report-2025) and [Swedish](https://www.momentum.group/report/ars-och-hallbarhetsredovisning-2025) at Momentum Group’s website [momentum.group.](https://www.momentum.group/en/) The Swedish version is also available in European Single Electronic Format (ESEF).

About Momentum Group

Momentum Group develops and acquires successful, sustainable companies. The group currently consists of some 35 companies that offer sustainable products, services and solutions for customers in industry and industrial infrastructure in the Nordic region. We are an active, long-term owner and combine the proven acquisition model and effective corporate governance of a 100-year-old industrial corporate culture with clear goals for sustainable development and long-term profitability at our companies. The group generates revenue of approximately SEK 3.1 billion and have some 900 employees. Momentum Group's B share is listed on Nasdaq Stockholm. For more information, please visit [momentum.group](http://www.momentum.group).
