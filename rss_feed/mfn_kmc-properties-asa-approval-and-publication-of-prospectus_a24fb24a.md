Oslo, 31 March 2026: Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement
published by KMC Properties ASA (the "Company" or "KMCP") on 9 February 2026
regarding the resolution by the extraordinary general meeting of the Company to
approve the statutory all-share merger pursuant to Chapter 13 of the Norwegian
Public Limited Liability Companies Act, with the Company as the surviving legal
entity and BEWI Invest AS ("BE-IN") as the transferor (the "Merger"), and the
contemplated offering in the Company (the "Offering"). The Offering will
comprise an offering to the public in Norway of no less than 120,000 and up to
400,000 shares in the Company (which following completion of the Merger will be
the combined company) (the "Offer Shares"), at a fixed offer price of NOK 25 per
Offer Share. The Offering is expected to raise gross proceeds of no less than
NOK 3 million and up to NOK 10 million.

The Financial Supervisory Authority of Norway (Nw. Finanstilsynet) has today, on
31 March 2026, approved a prospectus prepared by the Company (the "Prospectus")
for (i) the listing on the Oslo Stock Exchange of 114,943 new shares issued to
Bekken Invest AS following the private placement that was resolved by the
extraordinary general meeting of KMCP on 23 October 2025 (as adjusted for the
subsequent reverse share split in the Company, as resolved by the extraordinary
general meeting on 9 February 2026), (ii) the listing on the Oslo Stock
Exchange, or alternatively Euronext Expand, of 101,966,569 new shares issued as
consideration in the Merger (the "Merger Shares"), (iii) the offer and listing
on the Oslo Stock Exchange, or alternatively Euronext Expand, of the Offer
Shares. The Prospectus has been prepared in accordance with the EU Prospectus
Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2017/1129 of the European Parliament and of the
Council of 14 June 2017 and ancillary regulations, as implemented under
Norwegian law). 

The application period for the Offering is expected to commence on 13 April 2026
at 09:00 hours (CEST) and expire on 20 April 2026 at 16:30 (CEST), unless
extended. More information about the Offering will be provided prior to
commencement of the application period. 

Delivery of the Offer Shares is expected to take place on or about 24 April 2026
through the facilities of the VPS. Trading in the Merger Shares and the Offer
Shares on the Oslo Stock Exchange, or alternatively Euronext Expand, is expected
to commence shortly after completion of the Merger, on or about 24 April 2026.

Subject to applicable local securities law, the Prospectus, including the
application form for the Offering, will be made available at
www.danskebank.no/BEWIInvest, www.dnb.no/emisjoner, and
www.sb1markets.no/transaksjoner. 

Danske Bank A/S, NUF, DNB Carnegie, a part of DNB Bank ASA, and SB1 Markets AS,
are acting as managers for the Offering (the "Managers").
