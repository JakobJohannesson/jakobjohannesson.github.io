THIS NOTICE DOES NOT CONSTITUTE AN OFFER TO SELL OR BUY ANY OF THE SECURITIES DESCRIBED HEREIN.

Aktia Bank Plc ("**Aktia**") announced the issuance of Additional Tier 1 fixed rate notes with an aggregate amount of EUR 80,000,000 (the "**Notes**") on 25 March 2026. The Notes bear initial fixed interest semiannually paid rate of 6,75 per cent.

The Finnish Financial Supervisory Authority has today on 1 April 2026 approved the listing prospectus for the Notes. The listing prospectus is available in English on Aktia's website at [https://www.aktia.com/en/investors/debt-and-funding-strategy/funding/year/2026](https://www.aktia.com/en/investors/debt-and-funding-strategy/funding/year/2026).

Aktia has today submitted an application to Nasdaq Helsinki Ltd for the admission to trading of the Notes on the official list of Nasdaq Helsinki Ltd. Subject to the approval of the application, Nasdaq Helsinki Ltd is expected to admit the Notes to public trading as of 7 April 2026.

Nordea Bank Abp acts as the sole structuring advisor for the issue of the Notes. Nordea Bank Abp, Danske Bank A/S and ABN Amro Bank N.V. act as the lead managers for the issue of the Notes.
