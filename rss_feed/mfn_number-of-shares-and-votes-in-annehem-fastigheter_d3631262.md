# Number of shares and votes in Annehem Fastigheter

**Published:** 2026-03-31 17:45:00
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/beq/a/annehem-fastigheter/number-of-shares-and-votes-in-annehem-fastigheter-6395f9eb](https://mfn.se/beq/a/annehem-fastigheter/number-of-shares-and-votes-in-annehem-fastigheter-6395f9eb)
**MFN ID:** annehem-fastigheter
**Company:** Annehem Fastigheter

---

**According to Annehem Fastigheter's articles of association, owners of class A shares have the right to have such shares converted to class B shares. In March, at the request of shareholders, 3,000,000 class A shares were converted to class B shares.**

The total number of registered shares in the company amounts to 88,488,821 of which 7,295,986 are class A shares and 81,192,835 are class B shares. The total number of votes in the company amounts to 154,152,695.

**About Annehem Fastigheter
**Annehem is a long-term real estate company that owns, manages and develops sustainable commercial, community service and residential properties in the Nordic growth regions of Stockholm, Skåne, Gothenburg and Helsinki. The property portfolio consists of 27 investment properties with a value of MSEK 5,020.3 and a lettable area of 214 thousand sqm as of December 31, 2025. The company is listed on Nasdaq Stockholm (ANNE B) and is classified as a green share under the Green Equity Designation. For more information, please visit our website at [www.annehem.se](http://www.annehem.se/).
