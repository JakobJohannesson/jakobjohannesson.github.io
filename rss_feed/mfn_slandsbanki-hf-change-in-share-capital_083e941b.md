# Íslandsbanki hf. – Change in Share Capital

**Published:** 2026-03-27 17:28:00
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/a/islandsbanki/islandsbanki-hf-change-in-share-capital](https://mfn.se/a/islandsbanki/islandsbanki-hf-change-in-share-capital)
**MFN ID:** islandsbanki
**Company:** Íslandsbanki

---

At the Annual General Meeting of Íslandsbanki hf. (hereinafter the Bank) on 19 March 2026, the proposal og the Board of Directors of the Bank to reduce the Banks’s share capital was approved. The meeting resolved to reduce the Bank’s share capital by ISK 636,476,885 nominal value, equivalent to 127,295,377 shares, from ISK 9,402,353,850 to ISK 8,765,876,965 nominal value with one vote accompanying each ISK of nominal share capital.

On 25 March, the Bank issued a notice stating that an application had been sent to Nasdaq for the reduction in share capital, and that the reduction would take effect on Friday, 27 March 2026.

The aforementioned reduction has now been carried out, and it is hereby announced that the total number of shares in Íslandsbanki hf. as of today, 27 March 2026, is 1,753,175,393. Following the reduction, the Bank holds 21,861,379 treasury shares.

For further information, reference is made to the Bank’s announcements published in the stock exchange news system on 19 March 2026, regarding the results of the Bank’s Annual General Meeting, and on 25 March 2026, regarding the reduction of share capital.
