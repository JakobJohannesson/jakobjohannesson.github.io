**Attendo’s Annual and Sustainability Report for 2025 is now published at ****[www.attendo.com](http://www.attendo.com/)****.**

In 2025, Attendo took important steps and is well positioned to continue investing in both quality and capacity to meet the growing need for care in society. After a strong 2025 our profitability target for 2026 was reached ahead of schedule, and updated financial targets for 2028 were communicated in conjunction with the Year-end report for 2025.

“Attendo's ambition is clear: to provide better and more specialised care to more people – at a lower cost to society. Increased capacity utilisation, more accurate workforce planning and a continued clear focus on quality, together, contributed to this year's strong financial performance. As we look ahead, we do so with a strengthened financial position, a clear focus on our important social mission and high ambitions”, says Martin Tivéus president and CEO, Attendo.

Attendo’s strategy has always aimed at long-term sustainable operations. In 2025, a decision was made to join the Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi). Attendo has thereby committed to setting science-based climate targets. The purpose is to make it clear how the company is contributing to reduce carbon dioxide emissions in line with the Paris Agreement.

“Reducing carbon dioxide emissions is a prerequisite for long-term sustainable growth and profitability. The fact that we have initiated the process of adding climate targets is therefore a natural next step, where we grow and take responsibility for meeting future care needs in a sustainable way,” says Josefine Uppling, Communications and Sustainability Director, Attendo.
