# Bravida Holding AB: Bravida publishes Annual and Sustainability Report for 2025

**Published:** 2026-04-01 13:00:00
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/cis/a/bravida-holding/bravida-holding-ab-bravida-publishes-annual-and-sustainability-report-for-2025-251b2a9b](https://mfn.se/cis/a/bravida-holding/bravida-holding-ab-bravida-publishes-annual-and-sustainability-report-for-2025-251b2a9b)
**MFN ID:** bravida-holding
**Company:** Bravida Holding

---

**Bravida Holding AB (publ) has today published the Swedish Annual and Sustainability Report for 2025 on the company's website. The English version of the Annual and Sustainability Report will be published on or around 16 April 2026.**

"Bravida delivers a strong year-end result for 2025 with higher EBITA and an improved EBITA margin, although with lower net sales. The result is encouraging, particularly since the expected economic upturn never materialised. Our broad portfolio of services, combined with careful project selection, makes us a competitive and reliable partner for our customers - a partner who ensures that everything that has to work, works", says Mattias Johansson, CEO and Group President of Bravida.

The Swedish version of the Annual and Sustainability Report 2025, is available, via the following link: [https://investors.bravida.com/sv/reports-and-presentations](https://investors.bravida.com/sv/reports-and-presentations). The English version will be available mid-April via this link: [https://investors.bravida.com/en/reports-and-presentations](https://investors.bravida.com/en/reports-and-presentations)

For shareholders and other stakeholders who are unable to access the report digitally, a printed copy can be ordered by contacting Bravida via email to: [groupcommunication@bravida.com](mailto:groupcommunication@bravida.com)
