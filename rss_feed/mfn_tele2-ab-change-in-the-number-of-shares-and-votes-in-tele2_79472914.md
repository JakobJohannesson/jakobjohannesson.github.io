# Tele2 AB: Change in the number of shares and votes in Tele2

**Published:** 2026-03-31 07:30:00
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/cis/a/tele2/tele2-ab-change-in-the-number-of-shares-and-votes-in-tele2-48d878ff](https://mfn.se/cis/a/tele2/tele2-ab-change-in-the-number-of-shares-and-votes-in-tele2-48d878ff)
**MFN ID:** tele2
**Company:** Tele2

---

**Stockholm - Tele2 AB ("Tele2") (Nasdaq Stockholm: TEL2 A and TEL2 B) today announced that the total number of shares and votes in Tele2 have increased by 1,500,000 shares and votes, respectively.**

The change relates to 1,500,000 class C shares issued and repurchased in March 2026.

The purpose of the share issue and the repurchase is to enable the future delivery of class B shares to senior executives and other key employees participating in Tele2's retention and performance-based incentive programs. 

As of 31th March 2026, the total number of shares in Tele2 amounts to 697,721,597 of which 9,777,408 are class A shares with ten votes each, 685,344,189 are class B shares with one vote each and 2,600,000 are class C shares with one vote each. The total number of votes in Tele2 amounts to 785,718,269. The total number of votes in Tele2, excluding 1,565,465 class B shares and 2,600,000 class C shares held in treasury, amounts to 781,552,804.
