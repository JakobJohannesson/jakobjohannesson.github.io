# Vår Energi ASA: Correction: Notification of trade by primary insider

**Published:** 2026-03-30 12:35:35
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/cis/a/var-energi/var-energi-asa-correction-notification-of-trade-by-primary-insider-294edc3c](https://mfn.se/cis/a/var-energi/var-energi-asa-correction-notification-of-trade-by-primary-insider-294edc3c)
**MFN ID:** var-energi
**Company:** Vår Energi

---

**

Sandnes, Norway, 30 March 2026: Vår Energi ASA (the "Company") (OSE: VAR) refers to the stock exchange announcements published on 19 March 2026 and 23 March 2026 regarding the reported sales of 4 348 and 3 352 shares, respectively, in the Company by Deputy Employee Representative to the Board, Finn Volrat Pettersen.**

The Company has become aware that, due to an error, the transactions were incorrectly reported as sales and should have been reported as purchases. Further details are provided in the attached MAR notification.

Following these purchases, Finn Volrat Pettersen holds a total of 28 527 shares in Vår Energi ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to MAR Article 19 and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
