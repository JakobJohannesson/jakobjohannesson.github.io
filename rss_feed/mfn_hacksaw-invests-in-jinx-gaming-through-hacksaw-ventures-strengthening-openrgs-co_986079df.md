**Hacksaw Ventures, a part of Hacksaw Gaming, has entered into an agreement to invest in Jinx Gaming, a current partner studio on the OpenRGS platform, and has become a minority shareholder. The new agreement deepens the relationship between the two companies and supports continued innovation as they work together to develop and release new titles through OpenRGS. The agreement also includes the option for Hacksaw to increase its stake over time. Jinx Gaming joined the OpenRGS platform last December through the launch of its first title, Dead Headz.**

Through Hacksaw Ventures, Hacksaw invests in early-stage companies in its eco-system, including potential and existing OpenRGS studios. The focus is to identify companies and entrepreneurs with significant potential, where Hacksaw can contribute capital and knowledge to back these entrepreneurs, and help the companies become even more successful.

**Fran Mifsud, Vice President of Hacksaw Ventures, commented:**
*“Jinx Gaming has demonstrated a clear vision and strong creative direction since joining the platform and we are thrilled to welcome them as part of Hacksaw Ventures. The investment provides Jinx with further resources to develop innovative and exciting games. We’re excited to begin the next stage of our journey together.”*

**Reu Wilde, Founder of Jinx Gaming, commented:**
*“Out of the gate we’ve given players leading first-person-shooter and platformer experiences on the market, and now we have the might of Hacksaw behind us. Through our deepened partnership with Hacksaw, we will continue to rewrite the expectations of what a slot game is.”*

About Hacksaw
Hacksaw AB (publ) is a B2B technology platform and game development company. The scalable and modular platform, built on a modern code base, enables rapid development and distribution of games. Games developed by Hacksaw comprise digital slots, scratch cards, and instant win games. We operate across the whole B2B iGaming value chain, from game development to distribution and our customers comprise some of the largest private and state-owned iGaming operators in the industry. Hacksaw’s shares are listed on Nasdaq Stockholm (HACK).
