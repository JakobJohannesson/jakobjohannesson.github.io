General Oceans - Mandatory notification of trade 

Oslo, 1 April 2026: Reference is made to the announcements by General Oceans ASA
(the "Company") on 1 April 2026 regarding the exercise of the greenshoe option
to close out short positions created through the allocation of shares that were
over-allotted in the initial public offering of shares in the Company (the
"Offering"), and on 24 March 2026 regarding disclosure of large shareholding.

As part of the exercised Greenshoe Option, Atle Lohrmann has sold 2,421,121
shares and Ferd Go Holding AS has sold 2,578,879 shares. 

Atle Lohrmann (President of the Company) is a primary insider of the Company,
and Ferd Go Holding AS is a close associate to board member and primary insider
Gustav Martinsen.

Please see attached primary insider notification forms for the above-mentioned
transactions.

This disclosure is made pursuant to Section 4-2 and 5-12 of the Norwegian
Securities Trading Act and Euronext Oslo Rule Book II.

About General Oceans

General Oceans is a global provider of advanced underwater technology delivering
sensors, systems and robotic solutions used in demanding marine environments.
The Group produces technology for measuring and understanding the ocean, imaging
& navigation systems, and autonomous/remotely operated vehicles with associated
manipulators and control systems. Underwater acoustics is the core of the
Group's technology, complemented by optical technology, environmental sensors
and software capabilities.
