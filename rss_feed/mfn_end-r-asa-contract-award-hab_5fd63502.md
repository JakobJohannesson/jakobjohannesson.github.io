# Endúr ASA - Contract award HAB

27 March 2026 - HAB Construction AS ("HAB"), a wholly owned subsidiary of Endúr
ASA ("Endúr"), has today been awarded a new contract by Nordre Follo
Municipality, worth approximately NOK 50 million.

HAB will act as main contractor for the replacement of the existing Oppegård
bridge over the railway. The new bridge will include two traffic lanes and
widened pedestrian facilities adapted to surrounding infrastructure.
Construction is scheduled to commence in Q2' 26 and to be completed in Q4' 26.

About Endúr ASA:
Endúr ASA (XOSL: ENDUR) is a leading Scandinavian contractor headquartered in
Lysaker and listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange. The company specializes in
critical infrastructure, including bridges, quays, tunnels, and dams. Operating
as a disciplined serial acquirer (compounder), the group utilizes a
decentralized model that grants subsidiaries high autonomy to preserve their
technical expertise and local market positions. Endúr drives long-term value
through strategic platform acquisitions and targeted bolt-ons in fragmented
niche markets.
