# TF Bank AB (publ): TF Bank's subsidiary in the UK authorised by the Financial Conduct Authority

**Published:** 2026-03-27 21:30:00
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/cis/a/tf-bank/tf-bank-ab-publ-tf-banks-subsidiary-in-the-uk-authorised-by-the-financial-conduct-authority-1bf08eb7](https://mfn.se/cis/a/tf-bank/tf-bank-ab-publ-tf-banks-subsidiary-in-the-uk-authorised-by-the-financial-conduct-authority-1bf08eb7)
**MFN ID:** tf-bank
**Company:** TF Bank

---

TF Bank's UK subsidiary, TFBN Services Ltd, has been authorised by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) to carry on consumer credit activities and to provide payment services in the United Kingdom.

**About the Group**
We are a fast-growing provider of credit and payment services operating in 14 European countries.
