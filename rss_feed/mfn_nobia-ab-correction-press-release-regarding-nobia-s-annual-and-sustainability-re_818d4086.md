# Nobia AB: Correction: Press release regarding Nobia's Annual and Sustainability Report 2025 contained incorrect regulatory disclosure

**Published:** 2026-04-02 10:04:08
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/cis/a/nobia/nobia-ab-correction-press-release-regarding-nobias-annual-and-sustainability-report-2025-contained-incorrect-regulatory-disclosure-3ef75de9](https://mfn.se/cis/a/nobia/nobia-ab-correction-press-release-regarding-nobias-annual-and-sustainability-report-2025-contained-incorrect-regulatory-disclosure-3ef75de9)
**MFN ID:** nobia
**Company:** Nobia

---

The press release published on 1 April, 2026 incorrectly included a disclosure pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR). The correct legal basis for the disclosure is the Swedish Securities Markets Act (2007:528).
