# Peab publishes Annual and Sustainability Report 2025

**Published:** 2026-04-01 08:30:00
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/cis/a/peab/peab-publishes-annual-and-sustainability-report-2025-11d6784d](https://mfn.se/cis/a/peab/peab-publishes-annual-and-sustainability-report-2025-11d6784d)
**MFN ID:** peab
**Company:** Peab

---

**Peab's Annual and Sustainability Report 2025 is now available on Peab's websites. **

The Swedish version can be found at [www.peab.se](http://www.peab.se) and the English version at [www.peab.com](http://www.peab.com). The Swedish Annual and Sustainability Report 2025 is also available in European Single Electronic Format (ESEF). 

 

"Peab continued to build both local communities in the Nordic region and our own business in 2025. While demand for new homes was constrained, investments and activities in civil engineering, paving and public building construction shot up. We entered 2026 with a stable order situation and strong financial position - demonstrating once more that our broad business model creates endurable values for customers, shareholders and society.", writes President and CEO Jesper Göransson, stating that Peab is well positioned for the future.

 

The Annual and Sustainability Report includes Sustainability Statements in accordance with the requirements set out in the EU Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD).
