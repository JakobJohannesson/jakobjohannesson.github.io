JPMorgan Chase & Co. ("JPMC ") has a notifiable interest in the share capital of
Hexagon Composites ASA as a result of trades by its subsidiary J.P. Morgan
Securities PLC. The total number of shares held by JPMC on 26 March was
18,154,979 being 7.202% of the issued share capital and voting rights of the
company. The holding in JPMC crossed a notification threshold of 5% on 26 March
2026.
