**Neobo will publish the Interim Report for January–March 2026 at 07:00 AM (CEST) on April 22, 2026. CEO Ylva Sarby Westman will present the report and answer questions on the same day at 09:00 AM (CEST). The presentation will be held in English and can be followed via webcast and telephone conference.**

To participate via webcast with the opportunity to ask written questions, please use the below link.**
** 
[Webcast: Neobo Q1 Report (inderes.com)](https://neobo-fastigheter.events.inderes.com/q1-report-2026)
 
To participate in the telephone conference with the opportunity to ask verbal questions, please use the link below. After registration, you will receive phone numbers and a conference ID to access the conference
 
Call Access: [Neobo Q1 Report (inderes.com)](https://events.inderes.com/neobo-fastigheter/q1-report-2026/dial-in)
 
The presentation material and a recording of the webcast will be made available on the company’s website after the presentation.

About Us
*Neobo is a real estate company that manages and refines residential properties over the long term in municipalities with strong demand for rental apartments. Our vision is to create attractive and sustainable living environments where people can thrive and feel secure. Neobo’s shares are listed on Nasdaq Stockholm under the ticker symbol NEOBO and ISIN code SE0005034550.*
