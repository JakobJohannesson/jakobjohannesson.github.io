KALMAR CORPORATION, STOCK EXCHANGE RELEASE, 31 MARCH 2026 AT 6:30 PM (EEST) 

**Kalmar’s Board of Directors’ constitutive meeting 2026**  

Kalmar Corporation's Board of Directors elected by the Annual General Meeting has in its constitutive meeting elected Jaakko Eskola as Chair of the Board and Casimir Lindholm as Vice Chair of the Board.  

The Board of Directors considered all members to be independent of the Company and of its significant shareholders.  

The Board of Directors elected among its members Lars Engström, Marcus Hedblom, Vesa Laisi and Sari Pohjonen as members of the Audit and Risk Management Committee. Sari Pohjonen was elected as Chair of the committee.  

The Board of Directors elected among its members Jaakko Eskola, Carita Himberg, Casimir Lindholm and Emilia Torttila-Miettinen as members of the Personnel and Remuneration Committee. Jaakko Eskola was elected as Chair of the committee.  

The Board of Directors elected among its members Lars Engström, Vesa Laisi and Emilia Torttila-Miettinen as members of the Technology Committee which is a non-permanent committee. Vesa Laisi was elected as Chair of the committee.  

Ulla Bono, SVP, General Counsel, will continue as Secretary to the Board.

The company offers a wide range of industry shaping heavy material handling equipment and services to ports and terminals, distribution centres, manufacturing and heavy logistics.
