# Shareholding disclosure - Nordnet AB (publ) 2

**Published:** 2026-03-27 11:21:44
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/ob/a/lifecare/shareholding-disclosure-nordnet-ab-publ-2-efde9ecd](https://mfn.se/ob/a/lifecare/shareholding-disclosure-nordnet-ab-publ-2-efde9ecd)
**MFN ID:** lifecare
**Company:** Lifecare ASA

---

On March 27, 2026, Nordnet AB (publ), through its subsidiaries Nordnet
Livsforsikring AS and Nordnet Pensionsförsäkring AB, net sold 2,594,334 shares
in Lifecare ASA (ISIN NO0013355859). After these transactions, Nordnet AB
(publ), through its subsidiaries, held a total of 12,646,571 shares in the
company on a consolidated basis, representing 4.24% of the outstanding shares
and votes in the company.
 
The ownership distribution among Nordnet AB (publ) subsidiaries is as follows: 
Nordnet Pensionsförsäkring AB: 0.020%
Nordnet Livsforsikring AS: 4.223%

Nordnet AB (publ) does not exercise voting rights for these shares.
 
This disclosure is made in accordance with sections 4-2 and 4-3 of the Norwegian
Securities Trading Act and is subject to the disclosure requirement pursuant to
section 5-12 of the same Act.
