# Bravida Holding AB: Bravida is responsible for electrical installation work in Pågen's new bakery in Landskrona

**Published:** 2026-04-01 08:00:00
**Source:** [https://mfn.se/cis/a/bravida-holding/bravida-holding-ab-bravida-is-responsible-for-electrical-installation-work-in-pagens-new-bakery-in-landskrona-51f6992e](https://mfn.se/cis/a/bravida-holding/bravida-holding-ab-bravida-is-responsible-for-electrical-installation-work-in-pagens-new-bakery-in-landskrona-51f6992e)
**MFN ID:** bravida-holding
**Company:** Bravida Holding

---

**Bravida Finland has been awarded a contract by Tommy Allström Byggproduktion AB to carry out electrical installations at Pågen's new, state-of-the-art bakery facility. The project is one of Pågen's largest ever industrial investments.**

Pågen's new bakery covers an area of approximately 75,000 square metres and will provide an efficient production facility with a modern working environment. Bravida's assignment includes electrification of the production plant and the installation of, for example, security systems, process data centres and a backup power supply.

Jörgen Henriksson, Branch Manager, Bravida Finland, comments:

"We are a turnkey contractor for the project and are responsible for both the implementation of the contract and its comprehensive design. The project is complex and close cooperation with Pågen and other project partners is crucial for ensuring a successful outcome. We would like to thank Pågen and TAB for the confidence they have shown in the company and our skilful project team."

The first phase of the project is expected to be completed at the end of 2029. Profit and cash flow from the project are expected to be evenly distributed during the project's completion.
