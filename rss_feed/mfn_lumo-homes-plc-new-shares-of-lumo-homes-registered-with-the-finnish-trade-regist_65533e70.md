Lumo Homes plc Stock exchange release 2 April 2026 at 10:00 a.m. EEST**

**New shares of Lumo Homes registered with the Finnish Trade Register**

As previously announced by Lumo Homes plc, the Board of Directors of the company has issued 24,666,667 new shares in the company to Varma Mutual Pension Insurance Company as part of the consideration for the acquisition of the housing portfolio. Lumo Homes announced the completion of the housing portfolio acquisition and the share issue on 1 April 2026.

The total of 24,666,667 shares subscribed for in the share issue have been registered in the Finnish Trade Register maintained by the Finnish Patent and Registration Office today on 2 April 2026. Following the registration of the shares, the total number of issued shares in the company is 271,811,066, of which 7,000,000 are treasury shares. 

The shares will confer same rights as the company's other shares, including a right to dividends and other shareholder rights, as of their registration in the Finnish Trade Register and recording on the book-entry account of the subscriber. The recording on the book-entry account of the subscriber is expected to take place on or about 7 April 2026. Trading with the shares is expected to commence on or about 7 April 2026 under the trading code "LUMO".
